Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How accurate is your price estimator?

We report RMSE/MAE/R2 from a train/test split on our laptop dataset. Accuracy varies by configuration and market. See the About page for details.

How long does an estimate take?

Typically 1-2 seconds for a single estimate. For batch uploads, a few minutes depending on size.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We don’t store your form entries by default and use HTTPS. If storage is enabled, data is kept securely and never shared.

Can I integrate with other systems?

Yes. We expose simple REST endpoints for estimates. Contact us for API access.
